What we offer

100% pallet is a start-up that gives pallets a new purpose by creating fun and functional furniture.

A circular economy…

How do we work? You have useless pallets at the bottom of your garden or garage. You have neither the time nor the manual skills to transform them. Bring your pallets to us and our team will be able to propose you new furniture that we can built from them.

Create a totally unique piece of furniture…

As well as being great value, one of the best things about pallet furniture is that you can build furniture that looks unique and is exactly the size you need it to be.

Environmentally friendly furniture…

The trend for upcycling and repurposing everyday household objects is still in full swing and allows to give life to new objects which can look and be kinder to the environment.
